About Us

The Mind Building Youth Care Foundation is a non-profit organization located in Suleja, Abuja, Nigeria. Our mission is to nurture and empower young minds by providing support, education, and opportunities for youth to realize their full potential.

  • Founded by Mrs. Sarah Eseyi Oriowo, a passionate advocate for youth development.
  • Supported by her husband, Mr. Seun Oriowo.
  • Driven by the belief that every young person deserves a chance to succeed.
  • Committed to fostering a future where every youth is equipped with knowledge, skills, and confidence.

Mrs. Sarah Eseyi Oriowo

Chief Executive Officer (CEO) & Founder
Married to Mr. Seun Oriowo

Mrs. Sarah is dedicated to youth empowerment and community development. Her vision is to create a support system where young people can thrive, learn, and lead.

Our Programs

Mentorship Initiatives

Pairing youth with experienced mentors to guide their personal and professional growth. Our mentorship programs focus on leadership, career guidance, and life skills.

Caregivers Outreach

We support caregivers and families with resources, training, and emotional support to help them provide the best care for youth in need.

widows Visitation

Regular seminars and bootcamps on entrepreneurship, digital literacy, and self-confidence, tailored to inspire and equip our youth for the future.

Educational Support

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and tutoring for underprivileged students. We believe education is the key to unlocking potential and breaking the cycle of poverty.

Community Outreach

Organizing workshops, seminars, and events that promote mental health, leadership, and essential life skills. We engage with the community to create positive change.

Youth Empowerment Seminars

Regular seminars and bootcamps on entrepreneurship, digital literacy, and self-confidence, tailored to inspire and equip our youth for the future.
